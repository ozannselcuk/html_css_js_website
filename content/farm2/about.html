Dry Bulk

Shipping fruits and foods whether fresh or dry requires the highest level of care and a specific temperature control to preserve their taste, freshness, texture, and aroma. As Gw Trading, we follow highly standardized control procedures to guarantee optimal transport conditions from pick-up to delivery.

When you want to ship temperature-controlled goods, our access to all major reefer carriers ensures that your shipments will arrive on time and in perfect condition. For the transportation of perishable goods including fish, meat, dairy, fruits, and vegetables, we have a dedicated solution to our clients.

Our logistics services in ocean freight are;

  • Full container load – FCL
  • Less than container load – LCL
  • Import / export consolidation services
  • Port to port / door to door / warehouse to warehouse transportation
  • Combined expeditions
  • Transportation from third countries (cross-trade)

We also provide a full range of dry bulk vessel chartering and shipbroking services for both Suppliers, Buyers and Traders on the international shipping market. We are fixing dry bulk vessels on short or long-term time charter, on voyage basis or on long-term contract - Contract of Affreightment (COA) from 1,000 metric tons up to 50,000 metric tons.

Main commodities fixed are grain, fertilizer, various special fuels, salt, sugar, rice, wood pellets, wood chips and wood but also many more bulk commodities.

We assist for all the necessary dry bulk shipping operations;

  • Scanning the market to generate the ideal match between cargo and vessel.
  • Estimation of voyage.
  • Offering freights on basis of voyage, period and C.O.A.
  • Monitoring the vessel for compliance to charter party requirements.
  • Handling full post-fixture and account tracking.
  • Demurrage dispatch calculations.
  • Up to date market intelligence including port information.
