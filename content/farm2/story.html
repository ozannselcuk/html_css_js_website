About us

Who we are?

Gw Trading was established in the city Bursa, Turkey in 2020. The city is located in the strategic part of the Anatolian region of Turkey, in terms of the production of agriculture, fruits, and vegetable products and its short distance from the main seaports. Since the Company's inception, Gw Trading is a modern and dynamic trader operating in the field of Fruits, Vegetables, Frozen Edibles and Agricultural Commodities. We are continuously integrated with producers for the continuity of supply. Our trading activity ensures that we are always able to provide the full range of specifications of Agricultures whether for immediate or deferred requirements.

What we do?

During the recent global crisis that severely affected the food and agriculture industry for the last years, we have started our trading activities by taking behind Turkey's excess production of fruits, vegetables, and diverse agricultural products from all around of our region. We mainly export Turkish products to all over the world and we do this with much passion and drive. Also, we support farmers in our region to make their products known and improve their distribution in international markets. Also, according to the needs of customers, we supply biomass(biofuels) products in bulk that coming out of wood, energy crops and waste from forests, yards, or farms.
